05 Custom Parameter (Strange Attractor Inside!)
================================================

Here you can see an example of a custom parameter, how to reload results and use them for analysis.
The structure loosely follows the suggestions from :ref:`more-on-concept`.
We will simulate the `Lorenz Attractor`_ and integrate with a simple Euler method. We
will explore three different initial conditions.
